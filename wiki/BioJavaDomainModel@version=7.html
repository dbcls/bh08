Overview

Richard Holland and Richard Bruskiewich attempted to capture the BioJava object model in UML diagrams. These diagrams are presented on this page (a simply UML tool called  VioletUML was used...).

The end result is only a semi-compliant UML diagram for general discussion and comparison to other such diagrams (e.g. for BioPerl? and BioSQL) but is not computable (no XMI...)... Sorry :-)
